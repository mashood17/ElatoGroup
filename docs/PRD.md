# Executive Summary

ELATŌ is a premium hospitality destination that brings together handcrafted ice cream, artisan café experiences, elegant event celebrations, and premium serviced stays under one luxury brand.

The website serves as the digital flagship of ELATŌ and must communicate elegance, trust, refinement, and timeless luxury while providing visitors with a seamless experience across all services.

The platform consists of:

- Public Website
- Content Management System (CMS)
- Supabase Backend
- Media Management
- Booking & Inquiry System

The website must maintain a premium visual identity while remaining fast, accessible, responsive, and scalable.
